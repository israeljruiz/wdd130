Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to increase the sale of desserts made by Claudia's Bakery. This will be accomplished by informing those interested in the quality of these products along with how much they cost.

Audience

The audience for Claudia's bakery is anyone that would like to have a delicious dessert. Prices are not too much over what it costs to make the desserts so they are very affordable and within everyone's reach.

Content

Home page

People will come to my site because they are interested in learning more about the desserts I prepare and maybe learning a little more about Peruvian desserts. They will come because they wish to know the ingredients I use, how the desserts are prepared and how much they cost. They will come to the site to know how they can pay for the desserts and how they can physically get them. They will also want to see what the desserts look like and find a way to contact me.

Images for the Home page

Ingredients & Preparation

Visitors will expect to know what ingredients I use, along with how the desserts are prepared. Maybe visitors will wish to read the opinion others have of my desserts. Given that several of my desserts are Peruvian, maybe readers will want to know a little more about these ingredients and what Peruvian desserts are like.

Images for the Page 2

How do I order?

Activities visitors may wish to perform on the site could be placing an order, paying for the desserts, setting an address where they can receive them and finding a way to contact me directly. They may also wish to look at pictures of what I have prepared. Information that will be necessary for users to provide is email, phone number, name, address, payment method.
